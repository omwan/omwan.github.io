I'm a third year student at Northeastern University in the College of Computer and Information Science, pursuing a combined BS in Computer Science and Biology. I'm interested in creating and developing software, especially within a life sciences or medical context. Outside of programming, I enjoy playing the violin in the pit band for Northeastern University's musical theater group.

I'm currently on co-op at Novartis Institutes for Biomedical Research, within the Informatics division, where my role is developing software for cheminformatics research using Python and Flask. For my previous co-op, I worked as a software engineer at naviHealth, building web applications for post-acute patient care coordination, using Java/Spring MVC and AngularJS.
